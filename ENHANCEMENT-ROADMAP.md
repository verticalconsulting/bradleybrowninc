## ðŸ“ Notes & Considerations

### Decision Framework

When prioritizing enhancements, consider:

1. **ROI:** Will this increase revenue or reduce costs?
2. **Customer Impact:** Does this solve a real customer problem?
3. **Competitive Advantage:** Does this differentiate from competitors?
4. **Technical Feasibility:** Can we build/maintain this?
5. **Resource Availability:** Do we have time/budget/skills?

### Success Criteria

Each enhancement should have:

- Clear measurable goals
- Defined timeline and budget
- Assigned ownership
- Success metrics
- Rollback plan if needed
