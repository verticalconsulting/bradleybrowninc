# Bradley Brown Corporate

A professional construction and architecture theme that embodies trust, precision, and structural excellence, perfectly matching the Bradley Brown Inc brand identity.

## Color Palette

- **Corporate Blue**: `#003D82` - Primary brand color from logo, used for headers and key elements
- **Deep Navy**: `#002855` - Darker accent for depth and contrast
- **Steel Gray**: `#6B7280` - Professional neutral for body text
- **Light Gray**: `#F3F4F6` - Subtle backgrounds and section dividers
- **Clean White**: `#FFFFFF` - Primary background and contrast
- **Accent Blue**: `#0066CC` - Interactive elements and highlights
- **Sky Blue**: `#E6F0FF` - Soft highlight backgrounds

## Typography

- **Headers**: Montserrat Bold (or Arial Black as fallback)
- **Body Text**: Open Sans (or Arial as fallback)

## Best Used For

Construction presentations, project proposals, corporate communications, client-facing materials, architectural design decks, progress reports, and business development presentations. This theme conveys stability, expertise, and the precision of professional construction services.
